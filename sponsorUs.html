What is HackNA?

HackNA is a hackathon, bringing the joy of technology and problem-solving to high school students. In 2023's mini-hackathon, over the course of 7 hours, participants will create a project from scratch. Their project can, but is not required to, relate to this year's theme of Water and the environment.

Making a difference!

We decided to bring HackNA back for young students to give them to innovate: discover what they're capable of making. While many hackathons are geared towards adults or college students, our hackathon gives younger kids the opportunity to explore the engineering/technology field. HackNA 2023 will have a variety of workshops, catering to a wide range of students: from people who have never even heard of programming to seasoned experts.

Why sponsor us?

You can give back to the community. Your contribution would make this possible by fostering a diverse community of aspiring engineers.
Sponsoring us is also a great way to advertise your brand and interact with future innovators.
