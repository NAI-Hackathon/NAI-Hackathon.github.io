What is HackNA?

HackNA 2023 is a mini-hackathon, bringing the joy of technology and problem-solving to high school students. In HackNA 2023 at NAI, over the course of 7 hours, participants will create a project from scratch. Projects can, but are not required to, relate to this year's theme of Water and the environment.

FAQs

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?
A hackathon event where people interested in coding or learning coding complete a project in a short period of time to compete for prizes. No coding experience required!
WHO CAN JOIN?
People of ALL skill levels can participate HackNA! Over the course of the day, we'll have workshops geared towards beginners as well as ones for students with more experience.
COST?
NOTHING! We will be providing lunch, snacks, and courses throughout the duration of the event at NO cost to you!
WHAT SHOULD MY TEAM LOOK LIKE?
We recommend teams of 4 people, but don't worry if you don't have enough people! We can find you a team on the day of the event. You can also participate individually!
