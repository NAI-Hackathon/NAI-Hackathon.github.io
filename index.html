FAQs

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?
A hackathon event where people interested in coding or learning coding complete a project in a short period of time. This year's HackNA will be a mini-hackathon where participants will have 4 hours to make ANYTHING! No coding experience required!
WHO CAN JOIN?
People of ALL skill levels can participate HackNA!
COST?
NOTHING! We will be providing lunch, snacks, and courses throughout the duration of the event at NO cost to you!
WHAT SHOULD MY TEAM LOOK LIKE?
We recommend teams of 4 people, but don't worry if you don't have enough people! We can find you a team on the day of the event. You can also participate individually!
